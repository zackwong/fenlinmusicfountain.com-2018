Description

The music fountain is called the music performance fountain. It is a music control system based on the program-controlled fountain. The computer decodes and encodes the audio and MIDI signals, and finally outputs the signal to the control system to make the shape and lighting of the fountain. The changes are synchronized with the music, thus achieving the fountain water type, the combination of lighting and color changes and musical emotions, making the fountain more vivid and rich in connotation and water art.
