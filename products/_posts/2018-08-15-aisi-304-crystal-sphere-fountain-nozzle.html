Description

Crystal Hemisphere is a hemisphere effect nozzle. It is a large size nozzle that has sculptural and amazing water pattern like a crystal ball. The hemisphere effect is created by multiple discs of water at the radiating from a center manifold. These effects produce a fine spray that results evaporation cooling in the area of the effect. Crystal Hemisphere are water level independent and can be located in dry areas.

Crystal Sphere is a sphere effect nozzle. It is a large size nozzle that has sculptural and amazing water pattern like a crystal ball. The sphere effect is created by multiple discs of water at the radiating from a center manifold. These effects produce a fine spray that results evaporation cooling in the area of the effect.
