Description

Frothy foam jet fountain nozzle is made by full stainless steel 304 and plastic(plastic water fountain).The lively bubbling water pattern generated by the foam effect nozzles is the optimal accessory for watercourse inlets, cascading pool installations.

Fountain nozzle mixing air with water, the pattern appears to be extremely high volume. Nevertheless the nozzles actually have a low water requirement. The beautiful, soft foam jet offers a strong contrast to its environment.
