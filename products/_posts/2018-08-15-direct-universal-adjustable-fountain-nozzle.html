Description

Big Smooth Bores are the nozzles of choice in most stainless steel fountain, high visibility and excellent choreography performance is required. These nozzles are commonly used in dry-deck fountains and sequenced water effects mainly due to their instant reaction to choreographies and their perfect illumination capabilities.
