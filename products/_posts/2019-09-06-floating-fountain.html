Simple introduction of floating fountain

The floating fountain is also called the lake fountain. The pipeline system of the entire water fountain is installed on the floating tank. The operation of the fountain equipment is not affected by the seasonal water level. The pontoon is welded by steel plate or stainless steel plate, and the marine body paint is used for anti-corrosion treatment to ensure its service life.
