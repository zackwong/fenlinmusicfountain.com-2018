Rain Curtains are unique water features that give unique beauty to the place by allowing the water droplets to fall along with it like the indoor wall of water. They give amazing effects to the place where they are projected.

WHAT IS A WATER CURTAIN?

The out-flowing of water as the thin stream that runs continuously that looks like a waterfall. With little to no splash, these water curtain water features are nicely suited for urban landscapers and interior decorators.

USE OF RAIN CURTAINS

Rain curtains can be used in many ways as some decorators use them for the beautification of internals walls whereas some use them for the outdoor decoration of a building. They can also be used to decorate featured events like concerts, projection screens, conference rooms, and stages to create a particular, exciting effect of rain. It like a people dancing in the rain and you can call it dances {{ page.keywords }}. These curtains can also be used as a screen to view films and presentations.

WHERE THE WATER OF RAIN CURTAIN GOES?

Specially designed troughs are used to collect the water falling from a height through the strands of rain curtains. In fact, the whole system is a kind of closed system. The water collected in the trough is pumped to the top of the curtain to fall again through it. It can be used suitably indoors because it keeps it dry as very few droplets are supposed to fall on the floor in the whole process.
