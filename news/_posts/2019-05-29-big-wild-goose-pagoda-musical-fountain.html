Xi'an Big Wild Goose Pagoda Fountain is located at the North Square of Big Wild Goose Pagoda. It is 218 meters wide from east to west and 346 meters long from north to south. It is the largest square of Asian sculptures. There are two hundred-meter-long group sculptures, eight large-scale sculptures and 40 reliefs. It has the world's most luxurious green contactless toilets, keeping the cleanest, the world's largest bench, the world's longest light belt, the world's first direct water, the largest audio combination and many other records.

Fountain scale

According to the data, the water surface area of the fountain is 20,000 square meters; the eight-level inverter square in the eight-level stacked pool is the largest square array in the world. Each level of pool has 7 grades of water, which is printed on the 7th floor of the Big Wild Goose Pagoda. This set of fountains is designed with 22 independent water types. The frequency conversion array (the mountain water type), the lotus flower flower, the 100 meter frequency run spring, the sea bream, the seagull wings, the butterfly love flower, the water fire fog and the 60 meter high water jet column. In addition, it is the latest water-based new water type in China; four large-scale laser water curtains of 60 meters wide and more than 20 meters high are sprayed out of the water, and fully burned at a height of 6 meters. It has increased the popularity of the entire fountain. The music system uses the high-fidelity long-range professional sound system provided by Xi'an Sanxin Audio Company to make the fountain sound, light, water and color blend together.

The Big Wild Goose Pagoda North Square is designed with nine steps. Each step has five steps. Each level of the pool has 7 levels of water, which is printed on the 7th floor of the Big Wild Goose Pagoda. Step by step from north to south. The lights in the North Square are high-beam lights and plain lights. The spectrum analysis is very bright, insects are invisible, and there will be no insects flying in the entire square.

Safety guide

When watching the fountain, because of the large amount of water and high water spray, pay attention to protect the electronic products such as cameras to avoid being wet by water mist; children, pregnant women, the elderly and even adults should not be too close to the fountain spout when watching the fountain. Because the instantaneous impact of the fountain water is huge, you should prevent injuries!
