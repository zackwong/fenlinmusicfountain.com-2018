The musical fountain control system is an industrial field control system that integrates the international advanced scientific and technological achievements and integrates music control, program control and artificial intelligence control technology. The host computer is the multimedia industrial PC or computer musical fountain master, the musical fountain control system can realize real-time real-time sound control, and recognize the music melody, rhythm, music sense and audio strength. The music fountain system makes the music fountain Under the command of the computer, follow the music melody to constantly change the water type and synchronize with the music.

1. System performance indicators

(1) System multimedia software includes track management, music composition and multimedia presentation.

(2) Open structure, music arrangement skills are open to users, convenient and interactive The operation control interface allows the customer to select the music to play.

(3) Computer dynamic simulation and real-time monitoring system of music fountain.

(4) The same system has multiple working modes: manual control, program control, and sound. Music control, automatic timing control for unmanned operation.

(5) Using digital music signal processing technology to perform spectrum analysis and delay processing on music signals, Synchronize the fountain and music, synchronize the rhythm, and achieve a synchronization accuracy of 0.1 seconds.

(6) The control methods of the water pump include frequency converter speed regulation, multi-stage parallel connection, PWM digital control and switching quantity control.

(7) Due to the adoption of RS485 bus digital communication technology, the system can control the number of points of water pump, valve and lanterns to be infinitely expanded, with strong anti-interference and high precision.

(8) Songs can be played by CD, VCD, DVD player or computer.

(9) All fault control systems are used in the valve control loop.

2. System application range

The system can be applied to large square musical fountain , indoor stage fountains, hotel landscape spray Springs, park amusement fountains, corporate image fountains, residential community fountains, etc.

3. System matching method

Customers can choose any of the following systems as needed:

(1) Program-controlled fountain control system: it uses a timing program to change various water groups. If you add colored lights, it will become a color program-controlled fountain.

(2) Run Spring Control System: Run Spring is under the command of the computer music fountain master controller. The solenoid valves are sequentially opened and closed to control a long array of nozzles to produce a dynamic water shape. Running spring control. The controller can change dozens of patterns and change its speed. The fastest response speed is 20 seconds.

(3) Computer musical fountain control system: it is controlled by a computer music fountain master. Hanging down several computer fountain controllers, and a number of computer running spring controllers

Integrated control system.

(4) Computer Management Music Fountain Control System: It is an integrated control system consisting of a multimedia industrial PC as the upper computer, several computer fountain controllers and a computer running spring controller as the lower computer.
